Mision

At PILTZINTLI we seek to raise awareness and guide family members and patients with type 1 diabetes, providing the tools and knowledge to inform and cope with this disease, in order to improve their quality of life.

Vision

To be an association recognized at the local level for raising awareness and guiding family members and patients with type 1 diabetes, providing the necessary tools and knowledge from the hand of specialists and expert professionals in the field, to inform, promote awareness in society and cope with this disease in a better way.

Targets

  • Inform the society of the state of Aguascalientes through social networks.
  • Generate closeness with relatives and patients with type 1 diabetes.
  • Generate direct contact with specialists in the field.
  • Train students at the basic education level to generate a culture about this disease, breaking taboos, stigmas and stereotypes.
  • Provide advice and support.
  • Provide supplies to people in need.

Values

  • Commitment.
  • Responsability.
  • Tolerancy.
  • Empathy
  • Respect.
  • Honesty.
  • Solidarity.

Icon Meaning

  • BLUE CIRCLE: universal symbol of diabetes, symbolizes life, health and unity. HUMMINGBIRD: The hummingbird depends on nectar to live and its need, such that not consuming it for a few hours can be fatal. It's an analogy in that you're dependent on a high-sugar diet, whereas people with type 1 diabetes are insulin dependent. The word PILTZINTLI means “boy/girl” in Nahuatl and makes a fond reference to our children, since type 1 diabetes usually occurs in children, adolescents and young adults.
